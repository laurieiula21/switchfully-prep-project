Web development

I chose web development because it's the topic I know best, since I just did a training on it.

Front-end

The front-end is the part that everybody can see, it's the creative part, the styling...

The fundamental languages for the front-end, are HTML and CSS, and if you want something a little bit more dynamic, , there is Javascript.

There is also a long list of librairies and frameworks that can be very helpful designing a website, here is a sample of the most popular of them:

  • Bootstrap (CSS)
  • Tailwind (CSS)
  • React (Javascript)
  • Vue.js (Javascript)
  • Angular (Javascript)
  • And many others...

Back-end

The back-end is the part that makes everything work, it's the logical part

In the back-end there is much more options when it comes to language choice than in the front-end, there is PHP, Python, Java, Ruby, and even Javascript ! (and more...)

And just like the front-end, there are a lot of frameworks and librairies that can bus used in the back-end too, here is another little sample, this time, for the back:

  • Django (Python)
  • Express.js (Javascript)
  • Laravel (PHP)
  • Ruby on rails (Ruby)
  • Symphony (PHP)
  • And many others...
